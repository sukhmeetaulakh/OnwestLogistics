Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a shipment With OnWest Logistics? +
Booking a shipment with OnWest Logistics is quick and simple. Just fill out our online booking form with your pickup and delivery details, along with your package information. Once submitted, our expert logistics team will handle the rest. We offer seamless freight solutions for businesses and individuals across Australia.
What types of goods OnWest Logistics transport in Australia? +
At OnWest Logistics, we transport a wide range of goods, including general freight, refrigerated items, oversized cargo, hazardous materials, and more. Whether you need to ship locally within Australian cities or across the country, we offer reliable transportation for all types of shipments.
Can I track my shipment in real-time? +
Yes! With OnWest Logistics, you can track your shipment in real-time. Our advanced tracking system allows you to follow your freight’s journey from pickup to delivery, giving you full visibility and peace of mind throughout the process.
Does OnWest Logistics offer insurance coverage? +
Yes, we offer optional insurance coverage for your shipments. Our insurance options are designed to protect your goods from potential damage or loss while in transit, giving you added security and peace of mind during the shipping process.
How fast can I get a quote from OnWest Logistics? +
You can get an instant shipping quote from OnWest Logistics within seconds. Our smart quote system provides fast and accurate estimates, so you can quickly determine the cost of your shipment. Simply enter your shipment details, and you’ll receive your quote immediately.
